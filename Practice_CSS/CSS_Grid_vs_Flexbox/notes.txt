Grid vs Flexbox

GRID is a 2 Dimensional system          - we have control of how we layed out horizontally or vertically
FLEXBOX is a 1 Dimensional system       - control on how you lay out elements in column or row (one or the other)

GRID vs FLEXBOX   reference: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=18VLSXfsj94

FLEXBOX:
    * must either have a column or row based layout.
    * Do you wnat your conent to shape your layout?

GRID:
    * good for preceisly place grid items into cells defined by rows and columns
    * Do you want your layout shape your content?
